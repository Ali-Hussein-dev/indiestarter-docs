## What is a starter/boilerplate?
SaaS Starter Kit is a groundbreaking and comprehensive SaaS (Software as a Service) template created to simplify the development and launch of SaaS applications. This cutting-edge toolkit is meticulously crafted with a plethora of features, ensuring a robust foundation for your next SaaS project and beyond. Featuring an intuitive interface and a rich set of functionalities, SaaS Starter Kit empowers developers and entrepreneurs alike to swiftly kickstart their SaaS ventures without the hassle of starting from scratch. SaaS landscape, this comprehensive toolkit caters to your needs, providing a seamless development experience.

## What is Indie Starter?
Indie Starter Kit is a premium Nextjs starter designed to be minmial, but powerful stater point that can be used to build your next project. It comes with a lot of features out of the box, such as Supabase integration, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript, and more. It is designed to be a starting point for your next project, and can be easily customized to fit your needs.
